In the following form you can create accounts for new clients

Once the account is created, the customer will be notified via email.


By default the customer account must be active.

*The name is required.

*The last name is required.

*The E-mail is mandatory and valid.

*The phone is mandatory.

*Country is required.

*The company name is required.

*Description is required.

*Passwords must be the same (min 6 characters).
